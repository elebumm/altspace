Why altspace?

Place where you find yourself

Coworking is a proven solution around the world for hustlers working in the “grey area”. altspace hopes to serve the void and bring to Sarnia an incubator of new ideas, innovation and collaboration.

Meeting with Clients?

altspace members can meet with colleagues and clients at their desk or even in an enclosed meeting area.

Working from home?

Working from home can sometimes be unproductive. There has never been an opportunity like this in Sarnia, ON, a solution for small teams or solo operators. altspace members get to set up shop in a space that takes care of everything so they can focus on what they do best.

Starting a business?

Commercial leasing is complicated, long term and pricey... especially when you get into setting up workstations and designing an office space. altspace offers a low risk solution for consultants, freelancers, startups and more. Location and Culture

altspace is located in the heart of Sarnia’s downtown core, just a few minutes walking distance from our waterfront, bus terminal, local craft brewery, library and downtown’s cultural district. We know loving your work is important -- but where you do it is important too!

Culture is the most important part of our mission at altspace. We strive to create a common space where people are creative, respectful and innovative. altspace is more than saving money by sharing, it's being able to better everything we do by working together.

Space and Amenities

altspace is 3,000 square feet of industrial office space on the second floor of old Taylor's Furniture building. Members will have access to either a dedicated desk or flex desk as well as meeting space, lounge space and group collaboration space.

Our members will be taking advantage of a 300mbps download and 40mbps upload connectivity (fibre coming) as well as printers and other shared office supplies. altspace is position right above the Downtown Market so all members will have access to onsite cafeteria and dining area. The markets offering is growing and currently offers: cafe beverages, prepared food, deserts, kombucha and more!

FAQ.

How strong is the security?

Members with dedicated desks will each have a key lock drawer unit by their desk to securely store all personal and private belongings. The building as whole is armed with a security system as well as motion sensing cameras. All access is locked after office hours.

Will the space get noisy or messy?

altspace members must go through an application process to ensure each member is the right fit. We strive to create a space that ensures the safety and productivity of all members. That means quality control and regular maintenance at the end of each day.

What are the hours of operation?

When we begin our hours will be Monday-Saturday 9am-6pm, however these are subject to change.
